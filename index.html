LeagueVault Privacy & Support

Effective Date: December 18, 2025

Privacy Policy

  1. Information We Collect
    • Email Address: Collected during sign-up/sign-in. Used only for account authentication.
    • Fantasy Football Data: LeagueVault fetches public league, team, and player stats from the Sleeper API. No additional personal data is collected.
    • App Analytics: Apple collects app downloads, launches, and crash reports. LeagueVault does not collect additional analytics.
  2. How We Use Data
    • Email: To authenticate accounts and allow access to user leagues.
    • League Data: Display league history, player stats, career records, and matchups in the app.
    • Analytics: Used solely by Apple to monitor app performance; LeagueVault does not track or share analytics.
  3. Third-Party Services
    • Supabase: Handles user authentication. Stores only your email for login purposes.
    • Sleeper API: Provides public fantasy football stats. No other personal information is collected.
  4. User Tracking and Advertising
    • LeagueVault does not track users for ads, serve ads, or sell user data.
