LeagueVault Privacy & Support

Effective Date: December 18, 2025

Privacy Policy

  1. Information We Collect
    • Email Address: Collected during sign-up or sign-in. Used only for account authentication.
    • Fantasy Football Data: LeagueVault fetches public league, team, and player data from the Sleeper API. No additional personal information is collected.
    • App Analytics: Platform providers (Apple and Google) may collect basic usage data such as downloads, launches, and crash reports. LeagueVault does not collect or store its own analytics.
  2. How We Use Data
    • Email: Used solely to authenticate your account and provide access to your leagues.
    • League Data: Display league history, player stats, career records, and matchup information within the app.
    • Analytics: Used by platform providers only to monitor app stability and performance.
  3. Third-Party Services
    • Supabase: Used for user authentication. Stores only your email address for login purposes.
    • Sleeper API: Provides public fantasy football data. No private Sleeper account information is accessed.
  4. User Tracking and Advertising
    • LeagueVault does not track users for advertising purposes.
    • LeagueVault does not serve ads and does not sell or share personal data.
  5. Account Deletion
    • Users can delete their LeagueVault account directly within the app.
    • To delete your account, open the LeagueVault app, go to the Account Settings page, and select Delete Account.
    • Upon deletion, your authentication data (email address) will be permanently removed from our systems.
    • Fantasy league data displayed in the app is sourced from public APIs and is not stored as personal data.

Support

If you need help with the app, we can assist with:

  • Signing up or logging in
  • Connecting your league
  • Viewing league history or stats
  • In-app purchases (unlocking leagues)
